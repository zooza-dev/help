# Business Model Guides — Find Your Setup

Not sure where to start? Answer a few questions about how you run your business and go directly to the guide that fits.

---

## Who attends your classes?

### Children (parents book on their behalf)

| How do you sell? | Guide |
|---|---|
| Fixed block or term — parents pay upfront for a set number of sessions | [Children's Group Activities — Block / Term Model](./children-group-activities-block.md) |
| Monthly membership — same fee every month, classes repeat week after week | [Children's Group Activities — Monthly Subscription](./children-group-activities-subscription.md) |
| Short intensive — week camp, holiday programme, day camp | [Camps](./camps.md) |
| Private one-to-one lessons — tutoring, coaching, swimming | [Individual Lessons](./individual-lessons.md) |

### Adults (clients book for themselves)

| How do you sell? | Guide |
|---|---|
| Fixed term group courses and/or individual lesson packages — language school, tutoring | [Adult Language School](./adult-language-school.md) |
| Open schedule — clients choose which sessions to attend, pay per session or by pass | [Open Classes and Drop-in](./open-classes-drop-in.md) |
| Private one-to-one sessions — personal training, coaching, tutoring | [Individual Lessons](./individual-lessons.md) |

---

## Do you run classes online or in a hybrid format?

Any of the models above can run online or in a hybrid format. See [Online and Hybrid Classes](./online-and-hybrid-classes.md) for how to add meeting links, set up virtual locations, and include Zoom or Google Meet links in automated emails.

---

## Do you operate multiple locations or franchise branches?

If you have more than one branch, franchise licensee, or regional operator under one brand, see [Franchise and Multi-location Networks](./franchise-network.md) for how to connect accounts, centralise reporting, and manage client transfers between branches.

---

## Still not sure? Use this decision tree

**1. Is each client on a fixed recurring schedule (same class, same time, every week)?**
- Yes, and they pay per term → [Block / Term Model](./children-group-activities-block.md)
- Yes, and they pay monthly with no fixed end → [Monthly Subscription](./children-group-activities-subscription.md)
- No — they choose which sessions to attend → [Open Classes and Drop-in](./open-classes-drop-in.md)

**2. Is it one client per session (no group)?**
- Yes → [Individual Lessons](./individual-lessons.md)

**3. Is it a short intensive with a defined start and end (not a recurring weekly class)?**
- Yes → [Camps](./camps.md)

**4. Do your clients include companies paying for their employees?**
- Yes → [Adult Language School](./adult-language-school.md) — covers corporate booking and invoicing

**5. Do you have multiple branches or franchise locations?**
- Yes → [Franchise and Multi-location Networks](./franchise-network.md)
