# To-do List FAQ

## Can I see todos created by other team members?

Only if you are directly involved — either as the **creator** or the **assignee**. You cannot see todos between colleagues that do not include you. There is no company-wide to-do view, even for Owners.

## Will my colleague be notified when I assign a todo to them?

Yes. They receive a real-time notification in the Zooza sidebar as soon as you save the todo. If your company has Slack connected and the Todos stream is configured, they also get a Slack message. Notifications are not sent for self-assignments (when you assign the todo to yourself).

## What happens when the due date passes?

Overdue tasks stay open and move to the top of the list — nothing is closed or cancelled automatically. You need to manually complete or cancel the todo.

## Can I link a to-do to a specific booking or registration?

Yes. When you click **Add to-do** while viewing a booking, registration, event, or payment, Zooza automatically links the todo to that record. A context icon appears next to the task in the list — clicking it takes you directly back to that record. If you create a todo from the sidebar without being on a specific record, no link is attached.

## Can I attach files or write longer notes?

No. Todos support one line of text (up to 500 characters). There are no attachments, comments, descriptions, or subtasks. For longer notes, use the **Notes** field on the booking or registration itself.

## Can I create recurring todos?

No. Recurring tasks, reminders, and snooze are not supported. Each todo is a one-off task.

## Can I assign a todo to someone in a different franchise location?

No. Assignees must be users in the same Zooza company. If you need to hand off a task across locations, use the **Communication** section or a shared Slack channel.

## Who can delete a to-do?

Only the person who created the todo can delete it. The assignee can complete, cancel, or reopen it, but cannot delete it from both parties' lists.

## Can I recover a deleted to-do?

Immediately after deleting, an **Undo** option appears — click it to restore the task. After the Undo window closes, recovery is not possible.

## Why can't I see my to-do badge updating?

The sidebar badge and list update in real time. If updates are not appearing, try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, check your internet connection — real-time updates require a live connection to Zooza.
