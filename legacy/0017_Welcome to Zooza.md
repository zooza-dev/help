Individual lessons with 1st free lesson

Courses - individual lessons for one client, in which it is possible to sign up for the 1st lesson for free and then continue with the whole course.

Example: 

One-to-one English language courses with a free trial lesson. Then the client prepays for a package of 10 lessons with the option to retake.
