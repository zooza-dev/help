Notifications Center

The Notification Center is used to notify you as an administrator or your colleagues.

Set up notifications

In the 

Settings 

category, click 

Notifications 

and click on 

Add new notification

Select the type of notification from the list and type the email you want it to be sent to

Confirm your choice by clicking the

 Create Notification

 button

Note

: Only one email address can be entered per notification. For the next email you need to create a new notification.
